Completed ExcelDCF Financial ModellingMarch 2026
DCF Valuation Model — Rolls-Royce Holdings PLC

Built entirely from scratch using five years of Annual Report data (FY2020–FY2024). Projected revenue from £18.9bn to £25.5bn (FY2029E) using growth assumptions grounded in historical EBIT margins. Derived an intrinsic value of 471p per share — a 24% downside to the then-current market price of 620p.

  • WACC at 9.0% — applied both perpetuity growth (TGR 3%) and EV/EBITDA exit multiple (10×), blended to derive intrinsic value
  • Two-way sensitivity analysis across WACC vs TGR and WACC vs EBIT margin to stress-test the base case
  • Six sections: key assumptions, historical financials, revenue & EBIT projections, FCF build, DCF valuation, dual sensitivity table

Global Sales KPI Dashboard — BMW Group

Built a 4-page interactive Power BI dashboard analysing €31bn in BMW Group revenue across 4 regions and 8 models (2018–2025). Designed a star-schema data model with a custom DateTable and 6 DAX measures. Page 4 overlays GDP growth and fuel price index against unit sales — the kind of macroeconomic driver narrative expected in FP&A reporting packs.

  • 6 DAX measures: YoY revenue growth, total units sold, average selling price, BEV share, revenue YTD, units YoY%
  • BEV share grew from near 0% in 2018 to ~20% by 2025 — China and Europe leading electrification
  • X7 highest revenue model at 18.23%; macroeconomic correlation visible between 2020 GDP dip and unit sales decline
